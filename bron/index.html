lebron james

the goat

Here are some of his accomplishments

- A 4 time NBA Champion and Finals MVP with 3 diffrent NBA teams(2012,2013,2016,2020)

- Is a 13-time all-star with his first being in 2005 and his latest in 2024

- The all-time leading scorer in NBA history and ranks fourth in career assists.

- The first player in NBA history to accumulate $1 billion in earnings as an active player.

- He was selected by the Cleveland Cavaliers with the first overall pick of the 2003 NBA draft.

- He has won 20 ESPY Awards, hosted Saturday Night Live, and starred in the sports film Space Jam: A New Legacy   (2021).

- Selected to the All-NBA Team 19 times (including 13 First Team selections)

- leads the LeBron James Family Foundation, which has opened an elementary school, housing complex, retail plaza,        and medical center in Akron.
